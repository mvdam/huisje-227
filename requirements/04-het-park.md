## Observations

- This page is completely unmodified from the template. It shows English blog posts ("Travel Hacks to Make Your Flight More Comfortable", "5 Job Types That Allow You To Earn As You Travel The World", "30 Great Ideas On Gifts For Travelers") with lorem ipsum descriptions.
- All dates show "February 26, 2018".
- Content should be sourced from `website-content/park-facilities.md`, which provides comprehensive facility details including pricing for Adventure Golf, Fietsverhuur, and Pumptrack equipment rental.
- The park's general description emphasizes focus on children up to 11 years and their (grand)parents.
- Park address: Bongerdlaan 3, 1747 CA Tuitjenhorn, Noord-Holland.
