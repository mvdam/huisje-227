# US-012: Welcome Introduction & Video

**As a** visitor,
**I want to** read a welcome introduction and watch a promotional video,
**so that** I get a feel for the holiday property.

## Source

- [index.html](../../html/index.html#L159-L193)

## Feature Area

[Homepage](../02-homepage.md)

## Acceptance Criteria

- A welcome section with heading "Welkom!" and descriptive text is displayed
- A "Lees meer" button links to the "Het huisje" page
- A "Bekijk de video" link opens the property video in a lightbox
- Two images accompany the text (main image and accent overlay)
- All text, headings, and button labels are in Dutch
