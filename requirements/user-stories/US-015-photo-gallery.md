# US-015: Photo Gallery

**As a** visitor,
**I want to** browse photos in a carousel and view them full-size in a lightbox,
**so that** I can get a visual impression of the property and park.

## Source

- [index.html](../../html/index.html#L250-L520)

## Feature Area

[Homepage](../02-homepage.md)

## Acceptance Criteria

- A photo carousel displays property images with autoplay
- The carousel shows 1 image on mobile, 2 on tablet, 3 on desktop
- Clicking an image opens it in a full-size lightbox
- Users can navigate between images within the lightbox
- The carousel pauses autoplay when hovered
- Section heading and any captions are in Dutch
- All image alt text is descriptive and in Dutch
